At first glance, the potter wasp appears to be riding a broomstick. But this industrious insect isn't flying back to stir its witch's cauldron, or playing Quidditch. It was captured by macro wildlife photographer Bidyut Kalita and was among the runner-up or 'highly commended' entries in this year's Wildlife Photographer of the Year competition.
